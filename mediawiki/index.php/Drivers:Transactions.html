Overview

When a pay run is posted in the Packaging / Pay Runs screen, 'Payroll Deduction' records are created here and set at 'Pending' status.
When a pay run is receipted into the bank account, they are updated to 'Posted' status.

'Disbursement' records are created using a scheduler job.
Visit the Salary Packaging Scheduler Jobs page for more details.

The reports and email available here for one employee can be reported fin bulk from the Reports / Standard Reports screen.
Visit the Packaging Transactions page for more details.

[edit]

Legend

Legend Description
Pending Payroll Deductions - show as pending in this screen when they have been invoiced.

Disbursement - Claims and Payment records show as pending here when they have been posted.

Posted Payroll Deductions - show as posted when receipted in full and the receipts are posted.

Disbursement - Claims and Payment records show as posted here when they have been linked to a BECS or BPAY batch. Claims can also show as posted here if the record was raised in the Approvals Module.
These records do not have a payment method stored and are raised when the payment was performed off system.

History only History only records are included in the item's balance.
This status is used to show transactions performed off system or historical records migrated from other systems.
These records do not get selected in accounting exports.

Transactions are missing

Records in this screen are updated by a job running in your scheduler.
See also Salary Packaging Scheduler Jobs.
The job is called SP - Employees / Transactions
Check that this job is running correctly first, then contact your Account Manager for assistance.
