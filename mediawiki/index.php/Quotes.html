Quoting

The Quoting module allows you to enter lease and vehicle details and prepare a vehicle quote.

Quotes are managed by monitoring and updating the "Quote Status" of the quote throughout the process.

All quotes can be found on the Quotes / Search screen, but the default shows "Active" quotes (i.e. no Orders, Declined or Cancelled quotes).

To create a new quote, enter all the vehicle and client details.

At this point, the quote data can be used to email a quote request to a dealer for pricing, or you can use default system pricing.

In larger organisations, the pricing component may be managed by a procurement specialist.

  1. When you first save a quote, the default status is "Requested". This "Requested" status triggers an email to a procurement specialist.
  2. When the procurement specialist has finished pricing the quote, the quote status is changed to "Open" and the system sends an email back to you (the quote creator) and the Account Manager is sent a copy of this email.


When the quote pricing is complete, the Quote can be emailed to the Client or Driver. Alternatively, a PDF document can be printed out.

The normal system default is to send Novated Lease quotes to the Driver and Operating Lease quotes to the Client contact.

When a Quote has been accepted by the Client, the Quote Status is changed to "Accepted" and then converted into an Order.

Go to Fleet / Administration / Quotes to see detailed pages about Quotes.
