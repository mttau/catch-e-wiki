Overview

Create and update contacts here in detail.

Access existing contacts by locating them on the Clients / Contact List tab. Click click on the Surname hyperlink to come here.

When creating or editing a contact record, the "Given Name", "Surname" and "Email" fields are required.

If a client is part of a group, contacts can be linked to multiple client's in the Allocations section so their role for each client is easily seen.

The screen details details will be cleared when the "Client Code" is updated.

Action buttons

Field Description
Press 'New' to create a new contact.
Press 'Create' to retain any new contact details.
Press 'Edit' to update any existing contact details.
Press 'Save' to retain amended contact details.
Press 'Cancel' to abort the process and any entered or amended contact details will be lost.
