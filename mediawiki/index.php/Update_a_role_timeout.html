Main Page → Workflow → System Administration → Role Management → Update a role timeout

Qualifiers

  1. A role timeout is too long or too short

Process

  1. Go to System / Roles
  2. Role - enter or lookup the role you need to change
  3. Click
  4. Session Timeout
    1. Days - should be '0'. The only exception is the role 'fbt_declaration', where the session timeout holds the FBT Declaration Email open for the recorded time.
    2. Hours - should be '0'.
    3. Minutes - enter the duration the role can remain logged in without activity.
    4. Seconds - enter the duration the role can remain logged in without activity.
  5. Click
