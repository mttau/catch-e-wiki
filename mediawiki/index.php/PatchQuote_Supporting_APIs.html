Overview

The APIs shown below offer ways of obtaining data required for patchQuote. For example, patchQuote expects pay_cycle_id. Use getPayCycles to get the pay_cycle_id for your chosen Pay Cycle.

Supplementary SWAGGER documentation is available here: api.catch-e.com/docs/#/Quotes/patchQuote

These APIs are not configured for external use but may be made available on request.
Contact your Account Manager to discuss.
