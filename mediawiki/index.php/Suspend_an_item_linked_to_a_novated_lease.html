Main Page → Workflow → Salary Packaging → Items → Direct Link Car Benefits → Suspend a vehicle contract

Qualifiers

  1. This is a direct link vehicle contract
  2. The vehicle lease is ending


Item details

The item should not be edited or varied directly.
All updates to this item should be done in the vehicle contract

Update the last billing

  1. If the lease is ending early, confirm the final payroll date with the client
  2. Navigate to Contracts / Billing
  3. Click on
  4. Remaining Billings - reduce this so that the last billing date is the employee's final payroll date

Check and update pending records

  1. Navigate to Contracts / Payments
    1. Vary payment schedules to remove all pending payments (that you don't plan to pay)
  2. Navigate to Contracts / Maintenance
    1. Review and delete any pending claims and approvals that will not be paid

Raise the final billing

  1. Navigate to Contracts / Lease
  2. Click on
