Business Rules

  1. Clients / Contract Defaults "Periods Deferred" will default into Quotes / Finance "Months Deferred" for novated lease quotes.
  2. Contracts / FBT - the Transactions report will only print for novated lease contracts. The report is available to internal users to assist with contract reconciliations.
