Fuelcards

[edit]

I've put the fuelcard to the wrong contract

You will need to ask Catch-e support to move the transactions for you.
Before you do this, go to Fleet / Card Services / Cards and find the card that is joined incorrectly - link it to the correct Contract. Let us know

  1. the correct Contract ID
  2. the Card Number

We can find and update all the transactions that are not sitting against the correct contract. If you have a number of incorrect cards - put them in a spreadsheet.

Billing

[edit]

My invoice is wrong

You cannot delete sytem invoices yourself. However, Catch-e support staff can delete invoices for you - either completely, or only selected rows.
If only selected rows are deleted, the invoice can be re-printed and it will display and total without the deleted information.

If the invoice transactions are for lease budgets, these will be re-set to "pending" when the invoice is deleted. You can then correct the contract budgets as required.

In some cases, the Catch-e invoices are exported to your accounting system. If no exporting has been done, deleting invoices will remove those transactions from the future export. If the export has been done, you will either need to adjust your accounting entries to match the transactions in Catch-e, or delete the import and re-export from Catch-e.

If you want an invoice (or part) deleted, let us know

  1. how you are managing the accounting side of the entry (if already exported)
  2. the invoice number
  3. the contract number (if only one contract in an invoice needs deleting)

we cannot delete invoices that have been receipted. You will need to find the receipt and "unreceipt" the invoice. You can put the amount "On Account" until you have a correct invoice to allocate the money to.

[edit]

My billing started in the wrong month

Catch-e support can change the date of your lease records for you. This will not change the date that shows on the invoice, but it will;

  1. change the transaction date within the invoice
  2. change the date on the Contracts/Lease tab
  3. change which month the billing goes to on the Driver Report


Just let us know

  1. the contract number
  2. the correct start date for the lease
  3. the invoice numbers (only if you need to change the date of the invoices, but this is usually not necessary)

Approvals

How do I know if the Approval has been exported? The maintenance and approval rows are black (posted status) and the "Un-Post" button is not available.

How do I Edit or Delete an approval

  1. If the approval has been posted, click "Un-Post" to reverse the status to 'Pending'. You are now able to edit the approval.
  2. Just "Edit" and either make the required changes and "Save", or "Delete" the approval.

[edit]

I've raised an incorrect approval

1. If the approval has not been exported to your accounting system, you can edit the existing approval.

2. If the approval has been exported, create a reversing approval (to neutralise the original one) and then raise a new approval with the correct details.

[edit]

I've put an approval to the wrong contract

1. If the approval has not been exported to your accounting system, delete the approval and raise a new approval on the correct contract.

2. If the approval has been exported, create a reversing approval (to neutralise the original one) and then raise a new approval on the correct contract.
