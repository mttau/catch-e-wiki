Enrolled and Unenrolled 2FA from the Franchise portal

From Help wiki

Jump to: navigation, search

A new ‘2 Factor Authentication Enrolment’ button will appear on the Franchise Portal based on the following set up;

  • The [gb_roles] 2factor_auth_mandatory_flag = ‘no, and
  • The [gb_controls] 2factor_auth_enabled_flag to ‘yes’, and
  • The [gb_users] 2factor_enrolment_status_flag = ‘unenrolled’

In the Franchise Portal, where [gb_users]2factor_enrolment_status = ‘enrolled’, the ‘2 Factor Authentication Unenrol’ button will be hidden – not allowing the Franchisee to unenroll from the process.
This will need to be actioned internally by the Franchise Portal owner.
