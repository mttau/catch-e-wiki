Main Page → Workflow → Fleet Management → Claims → Post a claim created by a driver or employee

[edit]

Qualifiers

  1. This process is designed for Novated leases.
  2. Claims are being created in the Driver Portal and are saved at 'Requested' status.
[edit]

Post a claim

  1. Go to Approvals / Search
  2. The default search returns all claims at 'Requested' status, sorted by oldest to newest.
  3. Click on the first Approval hyperlink to navigate to Contracts / Claim
  4. Click on to open the attachments for review
  5. Click on . This verifies the data and runs the Funds Check
    1. If the contract does not have enough funds to post the claim, you will be alerted. The claim will be saved with a 'Pending Funds' sub status.
    2. If you have role access, the button will still be available and you can decide to over-ride the warning.
  6. Click on
  7. An alert will ask if you want to send a Reimbursement Notification email
    1. Click on to finish posting without emailing the driver or employee
    2. Click on to email the driver or employee. Doing this also creates a Reimbursement Notification event email record.
