Overview

Gap Insurance may be offered as part of a Comprehensive Insurance package, or it may be sourced separately.
Your Gap Insurance system settings need to reflect the way you are offering this product feature.

A "Gap Insurance" check box on the Quotes / Contract screen indicates if Gap Insurance is included in the lease or not.
If this is selected, quotes documents may itemise Gap Insurance as a product feature.

A "Gap Insurance" amount field is stored in the Broker section of Quotes / Finance and is used to store a cost (if there is one).
If an amount is entered, it forms part of the Brokerage amount that would be financed. This may also be used in settlement calculations.

Set-up scenarios and their behaviours

1. GAP insurance is included within a Comprehensive Insurerâ€™s policy and no default Gap amount is stored in the system
The Quotes / Contract "Gap Insurance" check box will turn on when an "Insurer" with Gap Insurance in their policy is selected.
The Quotes / Contract "Gap Insurance" check box will turn off and become disabled where the Insurer does not have Gap Insurance in their policy.
No "Gap Insurance" amount is shown in the Finance screen.

2. A default Gap amount is stored in the system
The "GAP Insurance" check box will be turned on regardless of the "Insurer" and the stored amount will be shown in the Quotes / Finance screen.

3. Both Comprehensive Insurers and a Gap amount are stored in the system (not recommended)
The "Gap Insurance" check box will be turned on regardless of the "Insurer" and the stored amount will be shown in the Quotes / Finance screen.

[edit]

How you can edit the Gap Insurance

  • Editing the amount of Gap Insurance

Quotes / Finance "Gap Insurance" amount can be edited, even if the Quotes / Contract check box is disabled.
Changing this amount from 0.00 to a higher value will turn on the check box.
Changing this amount to 0.00 will turn off the check box.

  • Turning the Check box ON and OFF

If Quotes / Contract "Gap Insurance" is checked, the gap insurance amount (if stored) will be updated and shown in the Quotes / Finance "Gap Insurance" amount field.
If Quotes / Contract "Gap Insurance" is un-checked, the Quotes / Finance "Gap Insurance" amount will be set to 0.

Set-up Notes

Insurers who offer Comprehensive Insurance with a Gap component are stored by their supplier code in [gb_controls]gap_insurance_included_by_insurers.
The default Gap insurance amount is stored in [gb_controls]gap_insurance.
