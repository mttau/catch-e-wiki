[edit]

Qualifiers

  1. The quote has been accepted by the Fleet Manger, Driver or Employee
  2. The quote documentation has been signed off


[edit]

Process

  1. Navigate to Quotes / Contract
  2. Click on
  3. If the Accept button is not available
    1. Click on
    2. Quote Status - 'Sent'
    3. Click on
  4. Click on
  5. Alert: - Click 'OK' to create a new Order from Quote '123456'!
  7. You are now in Contracts / Order
[edit]

Next Steps

  1. Order the vehicle
  2. Order a fuel card
  3. Activate the contract
