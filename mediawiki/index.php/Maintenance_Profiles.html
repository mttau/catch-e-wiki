Overview

Your system can be configured to calculation maintenance profile costs with the following logic:
Each variant that has a stored "Service Mths" and "Service Kms", these are used to calculate how many services will be expected based on the quote's "Term" and "Kms".
The variant is linked to a service profile that is suited to that kind of vehicle, E.g. (4Cyl Luxury 2WD 10K Service)
From this the estimated costs of the expected services return the "Total Budget".

See also Maintenance Profile Calculations model.

If you want to tune the budget that is calculated for a particular client, go to Clients / Quote Defaults "Maintenance Uplift Rate" and enter a rate (as a percentage) that will become part of this calculation.
This can be +/- to either increase or reduce the standard calculated rate.

An additional uplift can be applied for used vehicles. Go to [gb_controls]maintenance_service_uplift_rate and enter a rate (as a percentage) that will become part of this calculation.

The maintenance profile is re-calculated if you change any of the following fields:
Quotes / Contract "Effective Date", "Term(Months)", "Annual KMs", "Client Code"
Quotes / Vehicle "Variant", "New or Used", "Odometer".

Total Budget Calculations

[edit]
Example calculation if the vehicle is 'New'
  1. The vehicle has a service interval of 15,000 kms or 6 months. Every 7th service is a major one that costs 1,500.00. All other minor services cost 480.00.
  2. Quotes / Vehicle "New or Used" is set as 'New'
  3. The quoted vehicle has an assumed current odometer reading of 0 kms
  4. The calculated maintenance profile total will begin from service number 1 and it requires 8 services during its term.
  5. This is 7 minor services and 1 major service. The total cost will be $4,860.00


[edit]
Example if vehicle is 'Used' or 'Demonstrator'
  1. The vehicle has a service interval of 15,000 kms or 6 months. Every 7th service is a major one that costs 1,500.00. All other minor services cost 480.00.
  2. Quotes / Vehicle "New or Used" is set as is 'Used' or 'Demonstrator'
  3. The quoted vehicle has an assumed current odometer reading of 100,000 kms
  4. The calculated maintenance profile total will begin from service number 7 and it requires 8 services during it's term.
  5. This is 6 minor services and 2 major services. A total of $5,880.00

Set-up Notes

In most systems, these records are created by a scheduler job that is created during a client implementation.
Contact your Account Manager if you would like to review or make changes to the business rules that are used to do this.

  1. Each variant needs a linked record in [qt_variant_profiles]
  2. Each profile needs a linked record in [qt_maintenance_service_profiles]
  3. Each profile needs a stored set of service costs in [qt_maintenance_service_profile_intervals]

Useful Queries

Lists variants where there is no linked variant profile or maintenance service profile.

See also Maintenance Profile Calculations model.

select v.variant_id,v.short_description,vp.variant_profile_id,msp.maintenance_service_profile_id,msp.name
FROM qt_variants as v
LEFT JOIN qt_variant_profiles AS vp ON vp.variant_id = v.variant_id
LEFT JOIN qt_maintenance_service_profiles AS msp ON msp.maintenance_service_profile_id = vp.maintenance_service_profile_id
LEFT JOIN qt_maintenance_service_profile_intervals AS mspi ON mspi.maintenance_service_profile_id = msp.maintenance_service_profile_id
WHERE msp.maintenance_service_profile_id IS NULL
GROUP BY v.variant_id;

Lists each maintenance profile, the number of stored services and the total cost of all stored services. 

select msp.maintenance_service_profile_id,msp.name,MAX(service_no) AS services, SUM(mspi.service_cost+mspi.maint_cost) AS total_cost
FROM qt_maintenance_service_profiles AS msp
LEFT JOIN qt_maintenance_service_profile_intervals AS mspi ON mspi.maintenance_service_profile_id = msp.maintenance_service_profile_id
GROUP BY msp.maintenance_service_profile_id;
