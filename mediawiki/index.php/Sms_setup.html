The mailQuery service can be configured to send SMS messages

Another very usefull application of the mailQuery service is to send SMS messages.

Before you can send SMS messages from mailQuery you must first open an account with an SMS provider that offers an 'Email to SMS' service (most do). One such provider is SMSGlobal,

The process of sending an SMS via email is very simple, you enter the SMS text into the body of the email then send it to an email address containing the mobile phone number, e.g.

where the local part is the target mobile phone number and the domain part is the domain provided by the SMS Provider.
