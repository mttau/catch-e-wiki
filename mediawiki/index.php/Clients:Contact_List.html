Overview

Review or add a client's contacts here in summary.

Click on the contact's Surname to hyperlink to the Clients / Contact Details tab where additional details can be reviewed and entered.

When creating or editing a contact record, the "Given Name", "Surname" and "Email" fields are required.

If a client is part of a group, contacts can be linked to multiple client's on the Clients / Contact Details tab in the Allocations section so their role for each client is easily seen.

When the Client Code is changed, selected contact records are flushed from the contact details screen.
