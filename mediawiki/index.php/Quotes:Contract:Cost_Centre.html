Overview

Any text may be entered when quoting, but once a client is selected a cost centre may be linked.

[edit]

Require a Cost Centre

You can require a cost centre to be entered on a quote for a client.
Clients / Quote Defaults "Cost Centres Mandatory" - checked on

Where a cost centre is required, the user will be asked to select a correct entry before the quote can be saved.
The entry made in the "Cost Centre" field will be checked against Clients / Cost Centre records.

[edit]

Accept

When a quote is accepted, the stored "Cost Centre" is checked.
If the cost centre is not stored in the Clients / Cost Centre with an 'Active' status, an alert is shown.

The "Cost Centre" entry must be corrected or removed before the quote acceptance can be completed.
During the acceptance process, a Contracts / Events Cost Centre Change record will be created.
