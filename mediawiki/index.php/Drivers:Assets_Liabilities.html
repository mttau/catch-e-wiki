Overview

The Assets and Liabilities tab is used to create and display the assets and liabilities of the driver.
These records are used in the Angle Finance Interface and the Maclease Partner interface.
For all other financiers, continue to populate the driver's assets and liabilities in the Drivers / Finances tab.

Visit Employees / Assets & Liabilities for the employee version of this page.
