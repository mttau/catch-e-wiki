[edit]

Overview

Entering a suspend date affects a number of processes and is used in a number of business rules.
Below is a summary of where this date is used.

  1. Lookup - when you click on the icon and the search is for a contract, Suspended contracts are shaded in pink.
  2. Contracts / Header - if "Suspend Date" has been entered and "Terminated" is blank, the suspend date is displayed in the message row. E.g. Suspended 05/05/2018
  3. Contracts / Search - Suspended contracts are shaded in pink. Select "Include Suspended?" if you want to see suspended contracts in the Contracts / Search tab. If the suspend date is future-dated the pink shading is not done.
  4. Contracts / Order - if "Suspend Date" is blank and you try to update the "Contract Status" on this page to 'Suspended' or 'Terminated you will be required to enter the suspend date.
  5. Contracts / Contract - "Suspend Date"
  6. Contracts / Payments - entering a suspend date will trigger a data update to any active payment schedules to stop them. The stop date is the same as the suspend date.
    All pending records dated on or after the suspend date are updated to 'Stopped', any pending records dated before the suspend date remain as 'Pending'. If any records are 'On Hold', the 'On Hold' is removed and returned to 'Pending' or 'Stopped' as required.
  7. Contracts / Lease - if the records below are dated on or after the contract's "Suspend Date", they will not display or invoice from the Periodic or Combined billing screens.
    1. Periodic
    2. Adjustment
    3. Final
  8. Contracts / Lease - the records below will invoice from the Periodic or Combined billing screens regardless of their "Suspend Date".
    1. Credit
    2. Inertia
  9. Contracts / Terminate - if "Suspend Date" is blank and you try to update the "Contract Status" on this page to 'Suspended' or 'Terminated you will be required to enter the suspend date.
  10. Approvals / Header - if "Suspend Date" has been entered and "Terminated" is blank, the suspend date is displayed in the message row. E.g. Suspended 05/05/2018
  11. Approval / Approvals - users below manager level cannot create approvals for contracts once the suspend date is reached and are warned that it is suspended.
  12. Approvals / Contract - "Suspend Date"
  13. Adminstration / Reports - suspended contracts do not appear in reports unless the filters allow you to identify and include suspended contracts.
  14. Reporting / Standard Reports - suspended contracts do not appear in reports unless the filters allow you to identify and include suspended contracts.

Troubleshooting

Visit the main Troubleshooting page for a list of all the available problem-solving tips.


[edit]
Suspended contract is not billing

Contracts being billed on or after the "Suspend Date" are not selected for billing. To invoice a suspended contract, do one of the following

  1. Enter a billing selection date that is before the "Suspend Date". E.g. If the "Suspend Date" is '24/09/2021', then the billing selection date must be on or before '23/09/2021'
  2. Use the Contracts / Lease Invoice Now hyperlink to invoice the selected period (this will only work if the period date is before the "Suspend Date".
  3. Remove the contract's "Suspend Date", run the billing and then re-enter the "Suspend Date".
