Make a payment for one contract

From Help wiki

Jump to: navigation, search

Main Page → Workflow → Fleet Management → Payments → Make a payment for one contract

Qualifiers

  1. You want to make a payment for one contract
  2. All the required payment records have been created

Process

  1. Identify the contract and payment period you want to unpost
  2. Go to Contracts / Payments
  3. Payment Schedule(s)
  4. Locate the period that you want to pay. All pending payments due on or before the clicked row will be included in the payment selection.
  5. Due Date - click on the due date hyperlink
  6. Go to Payments / Periodic
  7. Click on
  8. If Delivery is 'Post'
    1. Review the PDF on-screen before the print is executed
    2. If you also wish to review or save the invoices as a CSV output before Posting, ALT TAB back to the main screen and perform a re-print to get a CSV file.
  9. If Delivery is 'Email'
    1. Review or discard the pop-up email queue
  10. Click on to confirm posting. This will update the payment records and create export data.
  11. If there is something wrong with the payment
    1. Click on Abort to back out of the payment
  12. If Delivery is 'Email'
    1. Go to System / Mail Queue
    2. Type - 'Payment'
    3. Status - 'Pending'
    4. Click on
    5. Click on
