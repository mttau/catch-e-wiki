Suspend an item - periodic

From Help wiki

Jump to: navigation, search

Main Page → Workflow → Salary Packaging → Items → Suspend an item → claim | periodic | recurring

Qualifiers

  1. An item will no longer be deducted from the Employee's pay because
    1. It has come to it's natural end
    2. The Employee has changed their packaging arrangements
    3. The Employee is leaving or has left
  2. The item's Disbursement Type is 'Periodic'


Process

  1. If the benefit has come to it's natural end, go to step 3. Suspend Contract
  2. If the employee's final pay has already been processed, go to step 1.a Remove all future pay run records
  3. If the employee's final pay is in the future, go to step 1.b Remove future pay run records that happen after the final pay run


1.a Remove all future pay run records
  1. Navigate to Contracts / Billing
  2. Click on
  3. Remaining Billings - update to 0
  4. Click on
  5. Make a note of the Life Budget Totals "Total"
  6. Go to the Finalise Payment Schedule step

or

1.b Remove future pay run records that happen after the final pay run

If the item finishes in the future and will still be on a few more pay runs, do this;

  1. Navigate to Contracts / Lease
  2. Period - open the list and locate the period that belongs to the final pay date. Count how many pending periods there are until including the final period. E.g. 2
  3. Navigate to Contracts / Billing
  4. Click on
  5. Remaining Billings - update to show the required pending periods. E.g. 2
  6. Click on
  7. Make a note of the Life Budget Totals "Total"
  8. Navigate to Contracts / Lease
  9. Check that the last pending period has updated to match the required final pay run date. E.g. 15/12/2021
  10. Go to the Finalise Payment Schedule step
2. Finalise Payment Schedule
  1. Navigate to Contracts / Payments
  2. Scroll to the bottom of the Schedule section and check that there are no records in 'Stopped' status
    1. If there are records in 'Stopped' status or the totals do not match
    2. Click on
    3. Stop Payments - un-check
    4. Click on
  3. Check to see if the Schedule Total matches the Contracts / Billing Life Budget Totals "Total"
    1. If the totals don't match
      1. Click on
      2. Total Payments - update the total payments to match what you need
      3. Click on
    2. If you need to create a part-payment to match the Contracts / Billing Life Budget Totals "Total"
      1. Click on
      2. Amount - update amount
      3. GST - update amount
      4. Click on
  4. When the final pay run is processed, the final payment will post when autoPostPayments runs. This will leave no outstanding payments.


3. Suspend Contract
  1. Navigate to Contracts / Contract
  2. Click on
  3. Suspend Date - enter the Final Pay Run Date + 1. E.g. 15/12/2021 + 1 = 16/12/2021
  4. Click on
  5. Go to the Finalise Payment Schedule step


4. Automated update by the Scheduler

A scheduler job should be set-up in your system called Update Item Data. This job does the following;

  1. When a contract
    1. has a Suspend Date entered
    2. has no pending billings
    3. is linked to an item with an 'Active' status
  2. the item is updated to
    1. record the final billing date as the "End Date"
    2. update the "Status" to 'Suspended'
    3. update the "Total Deductions" to show the Contracts / Billing invoiced "Total" (i.e. the total amount billed)
