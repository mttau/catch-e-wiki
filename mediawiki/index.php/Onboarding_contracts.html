Qualifiers

  1. You want to create contracts in bulk
  2. The contracts are for existing vehicles. E.g
    1. Sale and Leaseback
    2. Recording 'History Only' contracts to manage a client's vehicles as they fall due for renewal or replacement.

Variant Data

If you are able to populate either the red_book_code or the nvic_code and you have the vehicle data, you can back-fill variant data into the contract after creation.
See below for a sample query.

select cl.client_code
, c.contract_id, c.description, c.variant_id
, c.red_book_code
, c.service_interval_km
, c.service_interval_months
, '---->' as fm_contracts_import
, c.contract_id
, ifnull(v.variant_id,0) as variant_id
, ifnull(v.service_kms,c.service_interval_km) as service_interval_km
, ifnull(v.service_mths,c.service_interval_months) as service_interval_months
from fm_contracts as c
inner join fm_clients as cl on cl.client_id = c.client_id and cl.client_code = 'CLIENTCODE'
left join qt_variants as v on v.variant_code = red_book_code
left join qt_quotes as q on q.quote_id = c.quote_id
where q.quote_id is null
order by c.red_book_code, c.contract_id
;
