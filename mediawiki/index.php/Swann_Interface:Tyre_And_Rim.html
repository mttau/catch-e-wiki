Overview

The Swann Interface is a legacy product that is no longer offered by Swan Insurance.
Go to Tyre and Rim Insurance for the current product information.

[edit]

qt_tyre_rim_insurance_plans

Tyre and Rim policy pricing is held in the table qt_tyre_rim_insurance_plans. This needs to be populated for quote calculations to work. Otherwise, the pricing comes from the Global Control field qt tyre_rim_insurance.

The Swann Tyre & Rim interface may be turned on or off by updating the 'swann_interface_flag' in qt_tyre_rim_insurance_plans (yes or no).
