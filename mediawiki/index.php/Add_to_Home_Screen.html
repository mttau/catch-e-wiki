Jump to: navigation, search


Main Page → Driver Portal | Employee Portal → Add to Home Screen

Context

  1. You are a Driver or an Employee with an Apple mobile device (iPhone or iPad)
  2. You want to access the portal from your iPhone or iPad

Process

for iPhone
  1. Launch Safari on your ‌iPhone‌ or ‌iPad‌.
  2. Either
    1. Navigate to the portal site that you want to create a Home screen shortcut to or
    2. Type in the portal URL
  3. Tap the Share icon (the square with an arrow pointing out of it) at the bottom of the screen.
  4. Scroll down to the list of actions and tap Add to Home Screen
For Android
  1. Launch chrome on your ‌phone‌ or ‌tablet.
  2. Either
    1. Navigate to the portal site that you want to create a Home screen shortcut to or
    2. Type in the portal URL
  3. Tap the menu icon (three dots) at the top right of the screen.
  4. tap Add to Home Screen or Install app
