Receivables

The Receivables module enables you to receipt payments from clients and drivers directly into Catch-e. The receipt information can then be exported into your accounting package for debtor management and bank reconciliations.

The benefits of using Catch-e receipting:

  1. You can display a true cash position on Driver Reports
  2. Your allocations can be done at an invoice line level (this helps with Debtor reconciliations)
  3. You can monitor your finance payments against your debtor receipts

Current Debtor positions can be seen on the Receipt "Allocate" screen. Debtor aging reports are available via the Scheduler.

Channel Receipts

If you are a Channel Accounts user you can see all the related receipt batches that are associated with your channel.
