Overview

Use the Payments process to manage a schedule of regular payments to be made on a contract. E.g. Monthly Finance and Insurance payments.
Go to Contracts / Payments to create payment schedules.
Go to Payments / Periodic to post your payments each month in bulk, generate ABA files, review remittance advices or to forecast payments.
You can automate the posting process by setting up scheduled jobs to post selected payments automatically on the required date.

Visit the Contracts / Payments - Workflow page for step-by-step details on how to perform the various processes in this area.

Each Payment Type has a different business logic. Each page below describes how that payment type works.
