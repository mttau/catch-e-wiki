Overview

Two-factor authentication (2FA) is an extra layer of security for your Catch-e account, designed to ensure that you're the only person who can access your account, even if someone knows your password.

If your organisation requires you to use 2FA and you are not already enrolled, you will be navigated to the System / Users / 2 Factor Authentication tab after login to enrol.

Once enrolled, you may be able to chose to 'Remember' the second authentication, and this is defined by your organisation. E.g. 30 days.
If you 'Remember', you will not be asked for the second again until the 30 days have elapsed, or you login from somewhere else.
If you 'Remember' in Live, the details will be copied to Support. You will only be asked to provide 2FA in Support if your Live details have expired.
Go to the 2 Factor Authentication Setup page for set-up details.
