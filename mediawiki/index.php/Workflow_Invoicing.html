Main Page → Workflow → Accounting → Receivables → Create a post billing batch

Qualifiers

  1. Invoicing is due
  2. Sales Ledger and Journal export batches have been run
  3. The client does not require an invoice

Process

  1. Navigate to Fleet / Billing
    1. Select either Recharge, Periodic or Combined
    2. Delivery - 'Post'
    3. Dated - accept today's date or update to suit
    4. Click on Invoice
    5. View Reports - review and close (or save and close)
    6. - click to post the billing batch.
