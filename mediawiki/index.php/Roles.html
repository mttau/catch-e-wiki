Overview

Roles allows changes to Session Timeouts (Details) and KPI settings (KPIs) per role to be maintained.

Roles are also used in restricting or hiding particular system fields via our Role Restrictions table.

The following tables describe as a high level what access and edit rights each role is given by default.

Your roles may be different the what is documented here, as changes to roles can be changed to suit your business.

Also visit Claim Roles for a details listing of access to the Contracts / Claims tab.
