Main Page → Workflow → System Administration → Un-delete a deleted email

Qualifiers

  1. You have deleted an email and want to undelete it.
  2. The email does not need editing


Process

  1. Go to System / Mail Queue
  2. Filter the Mail Queue with status = 'deleted' to locate the email you want
  3. Click on the "Batch-Item" hyperlink to open the email window.
  4. Click on
    1. If you see the alert below, you will need to update the email address that is incorrect
    2. Alert: Cannot save due to the following invalid address:
  5. Click on
  6. Update your Status' filter to 'Pending'
