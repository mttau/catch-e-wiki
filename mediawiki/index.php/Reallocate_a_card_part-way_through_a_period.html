Reallocate a card part-way through a period

From Help wiki

Jump to: navigation, search

Main Page → Workflow → Fleet Management → Card Services → Re-enable deleted card transactions

Qualifiers

  1. You have imported and saved a fuel transaction file into a pending batch
  2. You want to split the transactions on one card over two contracts



Process

  1. Go to Fleet / Card Services → Transactions
  2. Batch No - enter or lookup the batch you are working on
  3. From - enter to filter only the first contract's pending transactions
  4. To - enter to filter only the first contract's pending transactions
  5. Click on
  6. Click on to post these transactions
  7. Clear the From and To dates. Now the second contract's transactions are displayed.
  8. Card Number - click on the hyperlink to open the Display/Edit Card screen
    1. Click on
    2. Contract ID - update the card record to reflect the second contract.
    3. Click on to post the batch
  9. Check and Post the remaining transactions which will then be allocated to the second contract.
  10. Click on
  11. The check process will update the "Validation" and "Status" of these records to match the updated contract
  12. Click on to post these transactions
