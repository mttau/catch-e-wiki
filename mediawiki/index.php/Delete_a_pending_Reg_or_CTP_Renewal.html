Delete a pending Reg or CTP Renewal

From Help wiki

Jump to: navigation, search

Main Page → Workflow → Fleet Management → Contract Actuals → Delete a pending Reg or CTP Renewal

Qualifiers

  1. You have a pending Reg or CTP renewal
  2. You are not going to make a payment for this transaction


Delete your pending record

  1. Go to Contracts / Maintenance
  2. Find the pending record to delete
  3. Reference - click on the hyperlink
  4. You will be navigated to the Maintenance / Reg/CTP Renewals screen
  5. Click on
  6. Find and check on the # check box for the record you want to delete
  7. Click on




Update your Batch

  1. Click on
  2. Target - reduce this value to remove the deleted record from the total
  3. Click on


Check your Batch

  1. Click on to verify that the uploaded registration numbers are valid and other data is valid.
The check will also run the GST Checks to make sure the "GST" amounts are within the tolerance range set in gst_tolerance.


Post your Batch

If ready, post your batch.

  1. Click on to post the batch.
Posting the batch will update the contract "Reg Expiry" date and "Reg BPAY Ref" fields (if "BPAY" is checked in the Batch).
If any contracts are currently being edited by other users than a warning message will be displayed.
The system must have exclusive access to the contract records in order to update the "Reg Expiry" date.
