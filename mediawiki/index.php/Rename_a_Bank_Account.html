Qualifiers

  1. You have a stored bank account that does not have an Account Name

Process

  1. Click on
  2. Click on the of the account you want to rename
  3. Click on
    1. Account Name - enter the new name.
  4. Click on or click on to discard the change
  5. Click on and return to the screen
