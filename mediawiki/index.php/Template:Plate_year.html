|qt||qt_quotes_plate_year_input|| Use this control to create a default year for the Quotes / Vehicle "Plate Year" field and to define the available years to show in the drop list.

Year or Range - specify the plate years by either year or range. Only one of these fields should have a value, the other should be blank. If both fields are populated, Year will be used.

Year - enter the earliest year you want to show in the "Plate Year" drop list in this field. "Plate List" will display all the years from Year to the current year.
E.g. If the current year is 2021 and Year is '2019', the "Plate List" drop list will display "2021,2020,2019".

Range - enter the number of years to show in the drop list.
E.g. If the current year is 2021 and Range is '5,' the "Plate Year" drop list will display '2021,2020,2019,2018,2017'. I.e. 5 records.

Lead Time - enter in days before the end of the year, when the next year should be shown in the list.
E.g. If current year is 2021 and Year = '2019' and Lead Time is '30' and today's date is 2/12/2021 the "Plate Year" drop list will display "2022, 2021,2020,2019".
E.g. If current year is 2021 and Range is '5' and Lead Time is '30' and today's date is 2/12/2021 the "Plate Year" drop list will display "2022, 2021,2020,2019, 2018, 2017". I.e. 5 records + extra year for Lead Time.

Default Year - enter a year to control the default value of the "Plate Year" field. When creating a quote in the system, this setting will be ignored if the stored year is outside of the years that will be shown in the "Plate Year" drop list.E.g.
1 . For the two examples above, if Default Year is blank, then "Plate Year" would show '2022' as the default.
2 . For the two examples above, if Default Year is '2021', then "Plate Year" would show '2021' as the default, but '2022' could be chosen.
3 . For the two examples above, if Default Year is '2014', then "Plate Year" would show '2022' as the default, and '2014' could not be chosen.
