Qualifiers

  1. An employee would like to package a novated lease or a managed associate lease
  2. You have created a vehicle quote for this
  3. An employee has signed off on the vehicle quote
  4. You have created a car benefit item linked to a vehicle quote (optional step)


Accept the vehicle quote

  1. Navigate to Quotes / Contract
  2. Click on
  3. If the Accept button is not available
    1. Click on
    2. Quote Status - 'Sent'
    3. Click on
  4. Click on
  5. Alert: - Click 'OK' to create a new Order from Quote '123456'!
  7. You are now in Contracts / Order
