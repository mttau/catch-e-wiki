BKCBCLCBooking / Fees & Charges incl GSTPremium Location Surcharge
Fuel and Damage Charges
BKCBKFCondition / Return Condition & Charges:Fuel Charge vehicle returned 'Fuel Status' (if the vehicle is returned Full, then no Fuel charge line is created, if a fuel charge is required, then the Fuel Status is displayed in the Invoice line. E.g. "Fuel Charge vehicle returned 1/4-Full".

The Fuel Charge is based on a Full Tank (say, $100) less the remaining amount (say, 1/2-Full). In this case, the Fuel Charge would be $50 (1/2 a tank).
Another example: The vehicle goes out 3/4-Full and is returned 1/2-Full - the Fuel Charge would be $25 (3/4-Full less 1/2-Full = 1/4 tank to charge).
The value of a full tank of petrol is stored in bk_fuel_charges.
