[edit]

Qualifiers

  1. This process is designed for Novated leases.
  2. This process requires the supporting documents to be in an electronic format. Scan and save paper files into pdf before starting this process.
[edit]

Raise a claim

  1. Go to Contracts / Claim
  2. Find and select the contract that the claim belongs to
  3. Click on
  4. Check BSB and Account Number details are correct
    1. If the bank account is populated, but isn't the one you want to use, follow the Select a bank account in a claim process
    2. If the bank account is missing, enter the details, review and check ON the Bank details correct? check box
  5. For each claim receipt, fill in the following fields;
    1. Date - enter or pick the date of the claim receipt
    2. Odo - enter this if known or leave blank
    3. Budget - select the budget that matches the receipt
    4. Classification - select the classification that best matches the receipt, this will be a default if no classification is required.
    5. Description - the classification will copy into this field. Tab over this or add to the Description if you want to keep more details.
    6. Qty/Litres - enter the fuel Litres if you have it, or a Qty for information.
    7. Total - enter the total of the receipt.
    8. GST - the GST will default in, check this and edit if it does not match the receipt
  6. Once all receipts have been entered, click on
  7. If any details are incomplete, you will be alerted and will need to update the missing details before you can save
  8. Click on
  9. Drop or browse to attach the scanned files
  10. Click on . This verifies the data and runs the Funds Check
    1. If the contract does not have enough funds to post the claim, you will be alerted. The claim will be saved with a 'Pending Funds' sub status.
    2. If you have role access, the button will still be available and you can decide to over-ride the warning.
  11. Click on
  12. An alert will ask if you want to send a Reimbursement Notification email
    1. Click on to finish posting without emailing the driver or employee
    2. Click on to email the driver or employee. Doing this also creates a Reimbursement Notification event email record.
