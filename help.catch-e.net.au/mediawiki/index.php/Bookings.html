The bookings module enables users to manage short-term vehicle hires using an internal fleet or vehicles sourced from third parties. Bookings requires a range of underlying data to be set-up for vehicles, locations and pricing.
Refer to Bookings Setup for a detailed explanation about how Bookings is set-up. Contact mailto:support@catch-e.com.au to get assistance with this module.

Bookings workflow

[edit]
Booking Request
  • Staff Member creates a booking request, detailing the Required Dates, Client, Driver, Vehicle Type and Accessories
  • On saving, the system sets a status of "Requested" and emails an alert to the Booking Administrator
[edit]
Allocation
  • Booking Administrator reviews the booking, sets the Billing Type and allocates a vehicle. This could be an Internal vehicle, Hire vehicle or Loan vehicle.
  • For internal vehicles, a pop-up allocation screen displays the internal vehicles that are available in the desired time-period, click to select.
* On selecting, the registration number and VIN fields are populated automatically from fm_contracts.
* On selecting, the fields to enter the PPS Reg No and PPS Reg Token become active.
  • For hire vehicles, the daily hire rate, extra charges and accessory charges are displayed.
* On saving, the system sets a status of "Booked" and emails an alert to the Booking Requestor.
[edit]
Start Details
  • For internal vehicles, an Opening Condition Report is entered into the 'Condition' screen. Details such as the Opening Odometer reading, Opening Fuel, Location and Condition are recorded.
* On saving, the system records the Odometer Reading in the maintenance table and creates a Location event if the vehicle Location is changed.
* On saving, the system sets a status of "In Progress"
[edit]
End Details
  • For internal vehicles, a Closing Condition Report is entered into the 'Condition' screen. Details such as the Closing Odometer reading, Closing Fuel, Closing Location and Condition are recorded.
  • A negative difference in the Fuel levels will result in a Fuel charge being calculated. This is in 1/4 tank increments. E.g. if the vehicle goes out 1/2 Full and is returned 3/4 Empty, then a charge for 1/4 tank of fuel will be calculated.
  • Vehicle damage can be marked on the vehicle diagram and a chargeable line is also created. The Booking Administrator can enter a Damage Charge at this point.
* On saving, Fuel and Damage charges are saved.
* On saving, the system sets a status of "Returned"
[edit]
Rental Invoices
  • For hire vehicles, the incoming invoices are checked and processed on the 'Rental' screen.
  • Supplier costs are stored in system tables and can be used to check supplier invoice calculations before processing.
  • Invoices can be processed as 'Interim' or 'Final'. 'Interim' would be used if the invoice will stretch over 2 or more months. 'Final' is for processing a one-off or final invoice. Extraneous charges such as Location charges and Accessories are billed on the final invoice when the start and end dates are confirmed.
* On posting, Purchase Ledger transactions are created and can be exported to an accounting system for payment.
[edit]
Client Invoices
  • Client invoices are generated on the 'Invoice' screen.
  • Like Rentals, client invoices are processed as 'Interim' or 'Final' invoices. Final invoices contain any extra charges that have been created in the Condition screen. (Fuel and Damage). If the Invoice is for a "Straight Rental" and a hire vehicle is used, the supplier costs for the rental are calculated.
  • Invoices can be created on the fly, these are known as a 'Single' invoice, or 'Billing' can be chosen. 'Billing' adds the booking charges to the clients next Combined invoice. A 'Recharge' option will be available shortly. A client preference can be set in the Client area for this.
* If "Billing", charges are processed with a "Billing" status (this is changed to "Posted" when the invoicing has been completed).
* If "Single", charges are invoiced with a "Posted" status.
* When a final invoice has been processed and invoiced, the system sets a status of "Closed"
