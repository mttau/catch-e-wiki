Overview

This screen is available for use in the Employee Portal and is a default option.
When selected, a user can change their contact details.

When logging in for the first time, users are asked to update and/or confirm their current details.

[edit]

Field Descriptions

Field Type Description [table]field
Client Text Displays your Employer's name.
Pay Cycle Display only Displays your pay cycle.
Employee No Text Helper text: Enter employee number
Position Text Helper text: Enter position
Cost Centre Drop List Choose your cost centre. Only those stored for your employer are shown.
If your employer is not using cost centres, this field will be disabled.
Annual Salary Number Enter your annual salary excluding super.
Allowances Number Enter any allowances you have.
Employer Super Number Your super will be calculated based on the Annual Salary you have entered.
Total Employment Costs Calculation Displays the total of the Annual Salary, Allowances and Employer Super amounts.
Employee Super Number Enter any additional super you are salary sacrificing.
HELP Debt Check Box Check this if you have a help debt.
