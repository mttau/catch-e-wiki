Transactions Report vs. Funds Check vs. Driver Reports

The Funds Check and the Transactions Report normally use the same report map and business rules.
They are similar to the Novated Driver Budgeted with Funds and Novated Driver Budgeted Operating with Funds reports, but there are some differences to be aware of;
