Main Page → Workflow → Fleet Management → Claims → Select a bank account in a claim

Qualifiers

  1. You are in the process of creating or posting a claim and
  2. You need to choose a bank account

Select a bank account

  1. Click on
  2. Move the over the Bank Account you want to select. It will be highlighted.
  3. Click on the record you want. You will be navigated back to Contracts / Claim tab.
  4. The bank account record will be populated
    1. If you can't see the bank account you need, click on to create a bank account
    2. If you don't want to change the bank account, click on to discard this record and return to the Contracts / Claim tab.

Create a bank account

  1. Click on
    1. Account Name - enter. This field is optional and can remain blank.
    2. BSB - enter
    3. Account Number - enter
    4. Claim - check if this account can be selected in the Contracts / Claim tab
    5. Default - check if this is the default account for claims raised in the Contracts / Claim tab or in the Driver Portal
  2. Click on to create the bank account record and add it to the item
