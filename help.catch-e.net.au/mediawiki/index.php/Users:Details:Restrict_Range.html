Overview

Restrict Range enables you to provide external users to a filtered combination of Clients, Divisions, Cost Centres and States.
Contracts and Drivers that meet the selection criteria are available for viewing and editing with the existing defined role permissions.

[edit]

Selecting "All"

Selecting All on one of the range parameters provides unrestricted access to the records that exist for the now and in the future.
E.g. if "All" is selected for the "Client Divisions" parameter, any divisions created in the future will be visible, whereas a line-by-line selection of all current divisions will limited the users access to only those selected divisions.

[edit]

KPI Charts

The set restrictions are also used on the KPI charts displayed on the Home screen for the user.
