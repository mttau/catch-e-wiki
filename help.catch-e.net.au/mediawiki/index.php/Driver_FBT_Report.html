Overview

The Driver Report can be

  1. printed individually from Contracts / FBT
  2. generated in bulk from Administration / Reports

The Driver Reports are designed to report the lease and FBT position of the contract at any point in time.

Driver FBT
Driver FBT Operating
Novated Driver
Novated Driver Operating
Novated Driver Budgeted
Novated Driver Budgeted Operating
Novated Driver Budgeted With Funds
Novated Driver Budgeted Operating With Funds


The content of the report depends on the nominated Contract Type of the vehicle.
When Contract Types / Details are set-up, templates are chosen for 'Statutory' or 'Operating' leases.
The appropriate report is invoked by looking to the Contracts / FBT "Benefit Type" setting. This is usually 'Statutory' or 'Operating'.
If the "Benefit Type" is 'Exempt', the FBT section of the report will display with N/A and 0 value entries.
The Report Date should be used to determine which FBT Value to use (fbt_value or fbt_reduced_value) but only if the fbt_reduced_value != 0.00 AND fbt_reduced_value_date != ‘0000-00-00’.

Report Timing

[edit]

Administration / Reports

Contracts commencing in the current reporting month are excluded from the Driver reports emailed. If the contract has periods deferred than the number of months deferred is also added to prevent reports being emailed until a reasonable time period, i.e. to reduce driver enquiry for reports with little or no transaction information - this rule is ignored when printing reports via PDF or CSV.
Vehicles that have passed their "Contract End" date are still included in the report selection.
Once a vehicle is suspended, it will not be included in the report selection regardless of the report date.

[edit]

Contracts / FBT

[edit]
Monthly

In this screen, you can select any reporting month contained within the "FBT Year Ending" year on the screen. I.e. April - March. Change the year to change the available reporting months.
The list is shortened when the "Contract Start" or "Contract End" are within the "FBT Year Ending" year. E.g. if the contract starts in June, only the months of June - March are available for the opening year.
You can run and email reports anytime from this screen, but they are only availabe for active or suspended vehicles. I.e. a "Contract Start" and a "Contract End" must exist for the contract.

[edit]
Whole of Life

When a contract contains a "Suspend Date", or a "FBT Liability End Date", a report option becomes available called Whole of Life.
The release notes showing examples of this option can be found here.
If the contract has an 'FBT Liability End Date' entered then the 'Whole of Life' driver report will take this date otherwise it will use "Suspend Date".

This report variation displays all expense and receipt transactions that belong to the contract, regardless of the transaction date. The monthly reports will only report transactions on or before the month end. This means any expenses, billings or receipts that are transacted after the month of suspension are not visible on the Driver Report, unless the Whole of Life option is selected.

On Whole of Life reports the "Life to Date" heading in the Budget vs. Actual section is replaced with "Whole of Life". The Whole of Life option is only available on the FBT screen to internal users, i.e. drivers and fleet managers cannot select this report.
Whole of Life is available on all reports except the two variations listed here; Driver FBT Statutory and Driver FBT Operating.

The Novated Driver Budgeted Operating with Funds report does not include some 'other' actual costs such as "Relief Vehicle" and Maintenance Program" fees. The Novated Driver Budgeted with Funds does include actual values such as these. Please contact your Account Manager for configuration advice.
[edit]

Delivery

The Delivery preference (Email, Post or Self Service) is read from the Drivers / Contact tab.

[edit]

Email

The email address is read from the Drivers / Contact tab. The system generates an email for each driver with a standard email body text that contains the PDF report. The PDF icon is a clickable link that will show you the PDF in a separate window (useful for checking before sending). The Novated Driver reports can be emailed individually or as a group.

[edit]

Email an individual report

  1. Go to Contracts / FBT tab for the chosen contract.
  2. Select 'E-Mail' from the format select box next to the 'Print' button.
  3. Clicking 'Print' will briefly show the message 'Building Report' then it should pop up the Send Email dialog box.
  4. Check the details and then click 'Send', if successful you get the message 'Sent OK!', otherwise 'Send Failed!'
  5. Close the dialog box with either the 'Close' button or the 'Exit' icon in the top right
[edit]

Email a group of Novated Driver reports

  1. Go to the Fleet/Admin/Reports Screen
  2. Select the required Driver Report
  3. Select Delivery = Email
  4. As at = (select a date, typically the end of the previous month once fuel transactions have been uploaded
  5. Contract Type: select only those starting with 'NOxx ' using the left mouse key or ‘Ctrl’ key
  6. Then click on GO. This will generate a list of emails.
  7. You can preview the individual reports by clicking on the pdf icon.
  8. Click Save (or cancel if you want to abort)
  9. Go to the System/Mail Queue menu
  10. Review the list in the mail queue and hit send if you are happy with it. Note that invoices also go through the mail queue so you need to be aware of anything else in the queue.

Post

The report may be printed to PDF or CSV.

Set-up Notes

Driver Reports emails are configured in the same way as other stored emails. This includes adding extra recipients and attachments

Email attachments may be converted to URLs if they exceed your set size limit.

A channel-specific email template can be configured to complement a channel-specific quote.
