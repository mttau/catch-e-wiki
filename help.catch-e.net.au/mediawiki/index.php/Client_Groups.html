Overview

Create a client group when the clients are related. this enables you to filter records by group on Search and Billing screens. You can also generate reports by group.

Billings can be emailed to a stored group email address.
There will be a separate attachment for each client's invoice because invoices cannot be issued at the group level, they must be invoiced to a specific client.

You can also set-up specific scheduler billing jobs to run for a selected client group.

For a nice way to navigate between related entity client records, visit the Parents and Children page.

[edit]

Using Groups

Clients / Contact "Group" - record the "Group code" in this field for each client that is part of the group.
Clients / Contract Defaults "Use Group Email?" - check on this check box if you want to email the client's invoices to this email address instead of the Clients / Contact "A/C Payable" email address.
Clients / Contact Details "Client Allocations" - allocate a client contact to multiple clients within a group here.
Mail Queue "Group Code" - use this field to filter and locate group email invoices.

A/C Payable Text Email address if sending billings to an central email address for a group.
External Id Text Display only field.
