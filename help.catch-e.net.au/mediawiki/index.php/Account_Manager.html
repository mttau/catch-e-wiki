Contact Support

Help Desk is available to all of our clients during business hours (9:00AM - 5:30PM EST).

  • mailto:support@catch-e.com.au
  • phone +61 03 9017 3520
  • http://www.catch-e.com.au/contact


Contact the Help Desk when:

  1. Have an outage, or believe your system is running slow
  2. The system is not working as expected (i.e. something is broken)
  3. There is a data problem and you need to delete or transfer records
  4. You would like to raise an new issue
  5. You would like to create or modify scheduled reports, emails or templates. Remember to attach the required Request form.

Tips for helping Catch-e to provide great support

1 Read the relevant Help wiki and Client wiki pages for answers first. Especially the Troubleshooting where we write up notes about how to fix common problems.
2 If you have a problem - you can never provide too much information!
3 Add screenshots whenever you can - include all of the screen in the shot. The URL details and the tiny text at the bottom of the screen can be very useful.

4 Where is the problem happening? If the problem happens to a particular record or field, provide the identifiers for this. E.g.

Quote ID will not save when...
Contract ID will not update when..
Client Code does not populate into...

5 How is the problem occurring? If the problem happens when you are completing a process with a few steps, tell us in as much detail as you can remember what those steps were. E.g.

  1. In the Reg or CTP Renewals screen I create a new batch
  2. Then I add ...

6 Do you have a theory? Have you analysed the problem? Let us know what investigations you've done and what your findings are. This can speed up the work.

Account Managers

Your Catch-e Account Manager takes care of your business needs and changes. Contact them when you need to:

  1. Change your system settings
  2. Ask for new features or system changes. Remember to attach the required Change Request form.
  3. Find out more about particular system features or set-up a new feature
  4. Discuss or get updates on your tasks

Users

[edit]

Credentials

To avoid experiencing problems with your credentials we recommend the following

  1. Keep a list of your logins in a Password Manager such as https://keepass.info/ to keep track
  2. Use the same password and update all environments when you change it - this will also help you keep track of the passwords.
[edit]

Password Resets and Unlocks

  1. In Live, use the Reset Password process to reset your password if it has been forgotten.
  2. The Reset Password process will not work in other environments, as they do not send emails
  3. For Support, Staging or Test, ask one of your System Administrators to check and update your password for you.
  4. If you are locked out, ask one of your System Administrators to unlock your account.


Issuing logins, resetting passwords and unlocking passwords for users is the responsibility of your organisation.
Requesting password resets from Catch-e Support will attract a charge.

How to UAT email changes in Staging

Catch-e Support will invite you to UAT new emails or changes in the Staging environment.
Emails will not send from the Staging environment, but you can check most of the email elements before you confirm it for Live.
URL links cannot be checked, so we recommend that these are checked when the email has been updated in Live.

  1. Go to a generated email (see a list of emails in the Email Setup page)
  2. Open the email pop-up and review the following
  3. The sender and recipients are correct
  4. The format and wording of the email is correct
  5. Open attachments and check they are generating as expected
[edit]

How to UAT Scheduler Job Reports in Staging

Catch-e Support will invite you to UAT new or updated scheduler jobs in the Staging environment.
Emails will not send from the Staging environment, but you can still check most of the details before you confirm it for Live.

  1. Navigate to the job you are testing
  2. Check that "Report Queue" is checked
  3. If it is not - Update a job to deliver reports to the report queue
  4. Run the scheduler job
  5. Go to System / Report Queue
  6. Filter and download the report

Scheduler & Ad Hoc Reports

  • Report Request - for new reports or changes to existing reports. There may be a charge depending on complexity of request.
