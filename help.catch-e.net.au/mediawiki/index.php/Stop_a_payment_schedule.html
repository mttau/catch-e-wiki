Stop a payment schedule

From Help wiki

Jump to: navigation, search

Main Page → Workflow → Fleet Management → Payments → Stop a payment schedule

Qualifiers

  1. You want to stop a payment schedule because
    1. The contract is an early termination or
    2. You want to edit the related fields that are disabled in the Contracts / Vehicle, Contracts / Insurance or Contracts / Finance tabs.
  2. When all payment rows are either 'Posted' or 'Stopped', the payment schedule is 'Inactive'.

Process - to stop

  1. Identify the contract and payment period you want to stop
  2. Go to Contracts / Payments
  3. Payment Type - select the target payment type
  4. Schedule - select the target payment schedule
  5. Click on
  6. Stop Payments - check
  7. Stop Payments - pick or enter the stop date you want. If the stop is temporary for editing, enter a date on or before the next pending Due Date
  8. Click on


Process - to re-start

  1. Identify the contract and payment period you want to stop
  2. Go to Contracts / Payments
  3. Payment Type - select the target payment type
  4. Schedule - select the target payment schedule
  5. Click on
  6. Stop Payments - un-check
  7. Stop Payments - the date will be removed automatically and the stopped payments will be restored to 'Pending' status.
  8. Click on
