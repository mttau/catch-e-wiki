You can use the Cost Centre screen to hold extra information about each cost centre. This is not used elsewhere in the system, but enables more detailed reporting at a client level.

Clients:Cost centre

[edit]

Field Descriptions

Screen NameTypeDescription[table]field
Cost Centre List Select the Cost Centre you want from the drop-down list, or it will be displayed if you have navigated here from the Cost Centres screen.[fm_client_cost_centres]description
Client Manager List Select the allocated Client Manager from the drop-down list of users. All active users are shown.[fm_client_cost_centres]user_id_cost_centre_manager linked to [gb_users]name
No of FTEs Num Enter the No of FTEs for this cost centre.[fm_client_cost_centres]no_of_ftes
Site Visits Num Enter the number of site visits required for this cost centre.[fm_client_cost_centres]site_visits
ContactText Enter the name of the contact person.[fm_client_cost_centres]contact_name
Job TitleText Enter the job title of the contact person.[fm_client_cost_centres]contact_job_title
PhoneNum Enter the phone number of the contact person.[fm_client_cost_centres]contact_phone
EmailText Enter the email address of the contact person.[fm_client_cost_centres]contact_email
Interests/CommentsTextEnter the interests of the contact person[fm_client_cost_centres]contact_note


This screen is hidden unless you want access to it. Contact Catch-e: mailto:support@catch-e.com.au or phone us on +61 03 9017 3520 if you wish to activate this screen.
This screen is enabled in the table gb_role_restrictions and needs to be configured by a Catch-e developer.
