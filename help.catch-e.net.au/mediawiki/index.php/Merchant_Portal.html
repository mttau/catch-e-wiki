Overview

Note: as at July 2025 the Merchant Portal is not active. Please contact Catch-e with reference OPS-22278 if you are interested in this feature.


The Merchant Portal is set-up separately of the main Catch-e system if it suits your organisation's needs.

Franchise users can login and create new approvals.

Approvals created in the merchant portal can then be viewed and updated by internal staff from the Approvals and Claims screens.
