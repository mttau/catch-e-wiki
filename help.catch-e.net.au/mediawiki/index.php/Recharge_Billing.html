Overview

Recharges may be processed any time during the month and are typically invoiced to clients at month end.

[edit]

Automation

We strongly recommend scheduling your bulk billings.Visit the scheduler billings page to find out more about how to do this. Your Account Manager can also assist you in setting this up.
This will be more efficient because

  1. Scheduled billings are 'set and forget'
  2. Emails are sent out in the scheduler process and do not require a second step
  3. Billings can be set to run out of hours, which reduces the likelihood of users being disrupted by the system locks that bulk billing creates. See Note.

Note - the billing process generates system locks that prevent users from editing records in many areas. Visit the System Locks page for full details about system locks.

[edit]

Recharge Transactions

Recharge transactions come from the following kinds of transactions;

  • Approvals / Approval records
  • Reg or CTP Renewals / Items
  • Card Services / Transactions

Recharge transactions are all shown on the Contracts / Maintenance tab.
Before invoicing, they are shown in blue text and have the following settings

  1. [fm_maintenance]recharge_flag is 'Y'
  2. [fm_maintenance]status_flag is 'recharge'
[edit]

Booking Charges

While not strictly a recharge, the Recharge invoices can also include Bookings / Invoice transactions.

[edit]

Fuel Recharges

By default, this screen uses the standard recharge invoice templates and selects all recharges that are ready to be invoiced except fuel.
Fuel can be added back to the selection if you only want to create one recharge invoice for your clients that cover all recharge items.
Fuel transactions are identified as having a Class of 'F'.

Otherwise, navigate to the Billing / Recharge → Fuel screen to create a separate invoice for the fuel recharges.

[edit]

Client Settings

If you cannot see a client's transactions on this screen, check which Clients / Contract Defaults "Billing Type" has been selected. Only recharges for clients with the selections 'Periodic' or 'Any' will be visible here.
