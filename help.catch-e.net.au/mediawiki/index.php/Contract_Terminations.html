Overview

The end of a contract, the lease needs to be reconciled.
If the contract is a novated lease, the shortfall is invoiced or refunded to the employer to pass back through their payroll to the driver.
Each of our client's will have a different process depending on the products they offer and how they are managed.

[edit]

Recommended steps to close down a contract

  1. Obtain a closing odometer reading from the driver. You can do this by either
  2. Record an "Odo" transaction in the Maintenance screen from some other driver documentation.
  3. Enter the "Suspend Date". Once a contract has a suspend date, it will no longer invoice or appear on reports (mostly, depending on the report requirements).
  4. Check for and cancel any active fuel cards attached to contract.
[edit]

Recommended steps to close down a Novated contract

  1. Make sure all necessary client invoicing and contract expenses have been entered and posted.
  2. Print the final Drivers Report and use the 'Whole of Life' feature to ensure all transactions are included.
  3. Create a new billing record to enter the variance amounts.
    1. If the final balance is a surplus (refund to the client), the inserted billing should be a credit (i.e. negative values).
    2. If the final balance is a shortfall (invoice to the client), the billing will be a debit.
  4. Check and post the billing (don't forget to un-suspend the contract for this step).
  5. When the Driver Report is reprinted, it should show a Budget / Actual Variance of 0.
