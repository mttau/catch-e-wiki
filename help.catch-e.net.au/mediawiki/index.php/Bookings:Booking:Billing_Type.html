The method of calculating a hire rate (Rate Daily Net or Rate Hourly Net) is governed by the Billing Type that is selected. When a booking request is generated, the requester specifies the 'Booking Type', this indicates the 'Billing Type' that should be used. 'Booking Type' does not contain any validations or business rules.

Billing Type Validation Internal Loan Car Hire Car Billing Calculation
Straight Rental n\a Internal Rates & Client Rates Rental Supplier Rates Rate Daily Net * Number of Days Used
Lease Replacement Requires link to Contract that is being replaced Free if Relief Vehicle, otherwise Internal Rates & Client Rates Rate Daily Net * Number of Days Used
Minilease n/aFree if Relief Vehicle, otherwise Internal Rates & Client Rates Rate Daily Net * Number of Days Used
Awaiting New Vehicle Requires link to On ORDER Contract Lease rate of vehicle On ORDERROUND((Quote Rate Net * 12) / 365, 2)
Free of Charge n/a Free n/a
