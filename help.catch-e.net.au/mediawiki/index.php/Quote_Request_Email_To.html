Overview

Use this feature if you have an internal process for vehicle pricing and procurement.
The emails below typically would go to and from a Procurement Officer.
To use the feature, enter an email in the "Quote Request Email To" field.
An email will be sent to this address when a quote is saved at 'Requested' status.
When a user updates the quote to 'Open' status, a return email is sent to the quote's creator.

[edit]

Requested Email

Sender: Quote creator
Recipient To: "Quote Request Email To" address
Subject: Quote Requested: {quote_id}
Message: Internal Comments: {internal_comments}

[edit]

Open Email

Sender: "Quote Request Email To" address
Recipient To: Quote creator
Recipient CC: Quote A/C Manager
Subject: Quote Ready: {quote_id}
