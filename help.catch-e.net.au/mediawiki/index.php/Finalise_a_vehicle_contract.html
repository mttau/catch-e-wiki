[edit]

Qualifiers

  1. The contract is for a novated lease
  2. You have suspended the vehicle contract
  3. All outstanding expenses have been posted
  4. You are ready to raise either
    1. A credit note to the Employer and return surplus funds
    2. An invoice for the outstanding final costs
[edit]

Process

  1. Locate the contract
  2. Navigate to Contracts / Lease
  3. Click on
  4. Depending on your processes, you can either let this billing select into the clients next invoice or process the final billing now as a separate invoice
[edit]

Invoice Now

  1. Next Date - click on the date hyperlink to navigate to the billing screen.
  2. Click on
  3. Click on to process the final invoice or credit note
