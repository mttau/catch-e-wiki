August 2025

(DEV-22328) ACT stamp duty changes 2025-09-01. Live 25/08/2025
As of 1st September 2025, the ACT Revenue Office has increased the Purchase Stamp Duty when registering a motor vehicle or transferring the registration of a motor vehicle in the ACT.
For more information, please visit the ACT Revenue Office website at https://www.revenue.act.gov.au/duties/motor-vehicle-duty
