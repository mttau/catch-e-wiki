Overview

The Master Lease Agreement (MLA) screen contains legal and contractual information pertaining to the client, such as client Insurance details, agreement dates and credit limits. Refer to the table below for additional information regarding each field on this screen:

[edit]

Header Buttons

Button Description
Click to create a new client. Three fields in the Clients / Contact tab are mandatory. These are: "Client Code", "A/C Manager" and "Sales".
Click to copy an existing client record. All existing record details except for the "Client Code" are copied to the new client record.
