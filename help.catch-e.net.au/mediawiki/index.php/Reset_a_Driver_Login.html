Reset a Driver Login

Main Page → Workflow → Fleet Management → Drivers → Reset a Driver Login

Qualifiers

  1. You want re-set login access for a driver that is locked out

Process

  1. Navigate to the Drivers / Contact tab
  2. Select the required driver record
  3. Click on the Access (Driver) check box (so it is un-checked)
  4. A pop-up window will ask you if you want to suspend access
  5. Click
  6. An alert will confirm that access has been suspended
  7. Click
  8. Click on the Access check box again (so it is now checked)
  9. A pop-up window will ask you to confirm you want to re-activate login access. This alert also displays the Password
  10. Click
  11. A pop-up window will confirm the login has been re-activated and will invite you to email the details to the driver
  12. Click
  13. An email window will display the email content
  14. Click
