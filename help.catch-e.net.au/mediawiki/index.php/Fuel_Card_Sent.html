Overview

When an "FC" contract event is created, an email message can be created.

The default email content is as follows:

Subject:
Fuel card sent
Mail Message:
Dear #given_name#

Contract ID: #contract_id#, Registration: #reg_no#

Your fuel card has been created and will be sent to you in the post today.

Kind regards,

Contact Catch-e: mailto:support@catch-e.com.au or phone us on +61 03 9017 3520 if you would like to change the content of your event email.

[edit]
Catch-e Set-up Notes

Use gb_templates to store the mail_subject 'Fuel card sent' and the mail_message. The footer is retrieved from [gb_controls]mail_footer.

[edit]
Placeholders

An additional placeholder is available for for use in the mail_message when ‘FC’ Fuel Card Sent contract event is created, as follows.
. #event_description#
