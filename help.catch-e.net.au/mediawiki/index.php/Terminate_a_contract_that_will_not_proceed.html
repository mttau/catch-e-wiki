[edit]

Qualifiers

  1. A contract record has been created
  2. The vehicle order is not proceeding
  3. No billing has been done
  4. No costs have been incurred


[edit]

When Start Date is blank

  1. Navigate to Contracts / Contract
  2. Click on
  3. Suspended Date - enter or pick today's date
  4. Terminated - enter or pick today's date
  5. Click on


[edit]

When Start Date is populated

  1. Navigate to Contracts / Contract
    1. Click on
    2. Suspended Date - enter or pick a date that is before the entered Start Date
    3. Terminated - enter or pick a date that is before the entered Start Date
    4. Click on
