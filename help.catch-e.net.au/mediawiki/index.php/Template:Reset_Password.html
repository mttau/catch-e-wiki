1. Go to the Login screen
2. Click on the Reset Pasword hyperlink
The hyperlink won't work if;
a) You no longer work at the company and your login has been suspended
b) The company uses single signon or Office 365 to access the system
3. Enter you User ID: and Email Address and click Reset
If you have forgotten your User ID and click on the Forgot User ID button, you'll see a message advising you to contact your System Administrator
4 The details you have entered are checked and matched to a stored record
If no match is found you'll see a message advising you to contact your System Administrator
5 If a match is found, an email is sent to you that contains a URL link
6 Click on this link
7 Enter your User ID: again
8 The Change Password screen will be displayed for you to change your password
9 On completion, you'll be asked to go back to the Login screen to login as normal
