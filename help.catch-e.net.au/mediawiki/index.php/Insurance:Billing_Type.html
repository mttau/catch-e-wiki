Overview

This field describes how the vehicle's insurance is billed and paid for. This field determines how a Novated contract's insurance costs are shown in the following reports:

  • Novated Driver
  • Novated Driver Budgeted
  • Novated Driver Operating
  • Novated Driver Budgeted Operating
  • Budget Km Variance


When the field "Billing Type" is set to 'Driver Reimbursement' or 'Client Reimbursement', the reports will show

  1. actual insurance costs from Contracts / Maintenance tab (i.e. where the client or driver reimbursed transactions are stored) and
  2. transactions from the Contracts / Payments tab in the "Actual" column and compare that to 'Budget' (the values from Lease screen budgets). Only posted Payment transactions will appear on the report and they are reported using their "Due Date", not the day the payment is posted.


Payment timing example - an Insurance transaction with a "Due Date" of 01/04/2010 was paid on 15/05/2010.

An April Report run on the 8th May would not include the transaction, because it would have been pending at that time.
An April Report run on 16th May would include the transaction in the "Year to Date - Actual" and "Life to Date - Actual" columns.


All other "Billing Type" choices ('Blank', 'Not Billed', 'Part of Lease' or 'Recharge') calculate the Driver Report Actual amount as the budgeted amounts plus any actual maintenance transactions for insurance. This allows any insurance excess amounts or actual to budget variances to be entered as maintenance transactions and reported accurately.
