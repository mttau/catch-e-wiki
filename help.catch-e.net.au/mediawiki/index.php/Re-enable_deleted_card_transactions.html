Re-enable deleted card transactions

From Help wiki

Jump to: navigation, search

Main Page → Workflow → Fleet Management → Card Services → Re-enable deleted card transactions

Qualifiers

  1. You have imported and saved a fuel transaction file into a pending batch
  2. You want to update deleted records



Process

  1. Go to Fleet / Card Services → Transactions
  2. Batch No - enter or lookup the batch you are working on
  3. Status - select 'Deleted'
  4. Locate the record you want to re-enable
  5. Card Number - click on the hyperlink to open the Display/Edit Card screen
    1. Check that "Posting Enabled" is checked.
    2. Edit and update this if it is un-checked.
  6. Repeat this for other records like this.
  7. Click on
    1. The check process will update the "Validation" and "Status"of these records to their configured values.
  8. Click on to stop at this point
  9. Click on to post the batch
