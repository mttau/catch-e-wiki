Contracts

The contract screens hold all the information and transaction data about a contract. A contract is usually for a vehicle, but it can also be for other assets that require ongoing monitoring and management.

Contracts are created when a vehicle needs to be ordered or an existing vehicle needs to be recorded in the system.

If the Quoting module is used, then "Accepting" a quote automatically creates an new contract record and copies all the necessary information from the quote. Otherwise, you can enter the information into the contract record directly.

When a new contract record is created, it is given a Registration Number of "ORDER" and displays in the Order Management screen and reports.

When the vehicle has been delivered, the "ORDER" registration then needs to be updated to show the vehicle's allocated Registration Number.

Go to Setting up a new Contract to see the recommended steps to set-up a new Contract.

The information held in the contract screens support a wide range of bulk processing areas such as client billing, supplier payments, card transactions, Reg/CTP processing and FBT declaration emails.

At an individual contract level, the Approval screen enables you to create and view vehicle approval records. It is usually used to provide approval numbers to suppliers (typically for maintenance & tyres), record cost estimates and process supplier invoices. It is also used for processing driver reimbursements.

At the end of a Contract's life, the Terminate screen is used to record and manage the vehicle termination in conjunction with "Due Off" and "Sale" screens and reports.

The default landing page for the Contracts menu is the Contracts / Search tab. This can be changed to be the Contracts / Contract tab by updating [gb_controls]contract_internal_user_default_landing_page from 'search' to 'contract'.
