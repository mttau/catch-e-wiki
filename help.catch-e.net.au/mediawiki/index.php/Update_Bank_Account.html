Update Bank Account

From Help wiki

Jump to: navigation, search


Main Page → Driver Portal | Employee Portal → Update Bank Account

Context

  1. You are a Driver or an Employee making a claim
  2. You are in the Banking Details screen

Process

  1. Click on Update
  2. You are now in the Change Banking Details screen
    1. BSB - Enter the new BSB
    2. Account Number - Enter the new Account Number
    3. Click on Cancel if you want to cancel this change
  3. Click on Confirm to make the change
