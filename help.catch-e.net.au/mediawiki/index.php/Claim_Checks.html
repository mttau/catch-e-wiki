Scheduler / Job

Job IDQuery IDParentTypeDescription
executeQuery Claim Checks

Run Time: Mon-Fri 7:15am EST
Selection Criteria:
Shows Item Claims where set-up may not be complete.
Claims that are dated in the next month are excluded, as they will fail the validation checks until they are in the current month.
A data check message is shown for each error.

  1. Claim is Active but Sub Status is not 'Pending Funds'
  2. Claim is Active but Sub Status is blank
  3. Claim is Active and Employee Bank Account is missing
  4. Claim is Active and Supplier is 'Employee Disbursement' and there is no attachment
  5. Claim is Active and Total Billings do not match Total Claims


[edit]

Data Check

1.1 Item is a fee - there should be no claims
1.2 Item is Recurring and pays a supplier - there should be no claims
2.1 autoPostClaims failed - Payee should be Employee - update then Check and then Post
2.2 autoPostClaims failed - Bank Account is missing - update then Check and then Post
3.1 Payee should be Employee - update then Check
3.2 Bank Account is missing - update then Check
4.1 Item is Recurring and Budget will not autoPopulate - Manually update billings
4.2 4.2 Contract is suspended. Review and Delete claim if it will not be paid

Process Page: Suspend an item

5.1 Sub Status should be Pending Funds - Check and then Post
5.2 Rounding bug - Check and then Post
5.3 autoPostClaims failed - Reason unknown - Check and then Post
5.4 autoPostClaims failed - Reason unknown - review and Check
6.1 Sub Status should be Pending Funds - Check
6.2 Sub Status should be Pending Funds - Check
7.1 No billing records exist to add amounts to - Contracts / Billings Vary
7.2 Billings and Claims Totals mis-match - investigate
8.1 Supporting attachment is missing
