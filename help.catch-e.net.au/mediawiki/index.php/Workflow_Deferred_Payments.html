[edit]

Overview

Finance Payments and Lease Billings can include deferred payments. If used, the number of payments and/or billings is reduced by the periods deferred. Any lease budgets (e.g. maintenance) then have their total budgets spread over the number of repayments.

[edit]

Set-up Notes

A default value to populate the Clients / Contract Defaults "Periods Deferred" field for new clients can be set in [gb_controls]fm_clients_periods_deferred_default.

[edit]

Configuration

Client preferences can be entered into Clients / Contract Defaults in the fields: "Periods Deferred", "Finance Budget Term Deferred" & "Budget Term Deferred".
"Periods Deferred" serves as the default value for Quotes / Finance "Months Deferred".
When "Finance Budget Term Deferred" & "Budget Term Deferred" flags are unchecked, this will spread the respective Finance and all other budgets respectively over the term.

[edit]

Quotes

A value stored in the Quotes / Finance "Months Deferred" field will reduce the number of finance payments required by that value, e.g. 1 or 2 is typical.
The Budget calculations are affected by a number of variables. Please open and view the Quote Finance Calculation for full details of these.
When the quote is accepted, the value of Quotes / Finance "Months Deferred" is copied into the Contracts / Finance "Finance Deferred" field. If the "Budget Term Deferred" flag is checked, the value of Quotes / Finance "Months Deferred" is copied into the Contracts / Finance "Billing Deferred" field.
If the Quote is applying an ECM calculation, using "Months Deferred" will also affect the value of the Post-Tax deduction in the first year of the lease. Visit the Employee Contributions page for a full discusion on this.

[edit]

Contracts

Contracts / Billing "Billing Deferred" is used in calculating the "Total Billings" of the contract and is also a default value when creating new Finance Payments.

[edit]

Deferral Discussion

Deferring Finance Payments can give you more time to enable you to receive funds from your customer and ensure a positive cash flow. Deferred payment arrangements would usually be arranged with a particular financier and applied to all leases with that financier.

Deferring Lease Billing can enable you to align the first invoice of the lease with the payment cycle of your customer. This is particularly useful with Novated Leases where the payments need to be processed through the Driver’s pay. It is less relevant to Operating Leases, where the end customer can comfortably make multiple payments to catch-up. Deferring Lease Billing can be done as a policy within your organisation, or can be set or varied by client to suit each client’s processing timeframes. If there is no deferral, the client may always be behind in payments, if the deferral set is too long, the client might be making payments that cannot be allocated.

When determining the use of deferred payments, consider the following.

  • The alignment of Finance Payment deferrals and Lease Payment deferrals so that cash flow is positive.
  • The timing of the last payments and the Lease End to make sure all payment obligations are met at or before the end of the Lease term.
  • How the Lease Billing deferral is communicated to the Client/HR department.
  • How the Lease Billing deferral will affect (and be displayed) on your Driver Reports.
  • How the Lease Billing deferral affects the calculation of early termination payouts.
  • How the Lease Billing deferral and Finance Payments will be treated and reconciled in your accounts.
